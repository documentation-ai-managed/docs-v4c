## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A GitHub account for importing repositories

</Callout>

No software installation required—V4c runs entirely in your browser.

## Sign Up and Log In

Create your V4c account in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit V4c" icon="globe">
    Open [https://app.v4c.com](https://app.v4c.com) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** and enter your email, password, and display name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the login page and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
  You'll land on the V4c dashboard, ready to create your first space.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Spaces organize your project docs. Follow these steps to set one up.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name It" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and select a visibility (Public or Private).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize" icon="settings">
    Add a description and choose the brand color `#3B82F6`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Create Space**. It appears in your dashboard instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
