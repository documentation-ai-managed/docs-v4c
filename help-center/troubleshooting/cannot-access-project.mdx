---
title: Cannot Access Project
description: Fix access issues with your V4c project.
---

## Symptoms

- "Access Denied" error when opening a doc or project
- Missing projects from dashboard

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your permissions">
    Ensure you are added as a collaborator by the project admin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Log out, clear your cache, and log in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact admin or support">
    If the issue continues, contact your project admin or email support@v4c.io
  </Step>
</Steps>
